# Water Leakage and Flooding Response SOP

Team: Engineering & Maintenance
Severity: High or Critical

1. Acknowledge the guest within five minutes and ask them to move away from electrical points.
2. Dispatch engineering with leak-containment equipment and alert housekeeping for water removal.
3. Shut the local water valve when safe. If water approaches electrical fittings, isolate the circuit and escalate to the Manager on Duty.
4. Offer a room move when repair cannot be completed within twenty minutes.
5. Record photographs, root cause, parts used, guest recovery action, and closure confirmation.
